Today, in the 4.0 era, the amount of information a person receives in a day is increasing day by day. However, each person's time is only 24 hours, so it is extremely important to grasp the content of the text or information that we are about to receive. For that reason, the need for text summaries has also increased. Text summaries condense a text to its main points, saving us time and helping us to see if the text's outline is necessary and relevant to us.


What is Text Summarization?

Text summarization is the process of turning larger documents into shorter and precise paragraphs or sentences. The process brings out information that is crucial, and also ensures that the meaning of the paragraph stays the same. This helps reduce the time to understand large papers like research articles, without skipping any vital information.


According to the Oxford dictionary, the Text Summary is:

a short statement that gives only the main points of something, not the details.
